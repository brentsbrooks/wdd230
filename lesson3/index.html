Centurion Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

The site is going to be called Centurion Chamber of Commerce

Domain Name

The domain name for the website is centurionchamberofcommerce.co.za

Site Purpose

The purpose of the website for the Centurion Chamber of commerce is to bring together businesses and businesspeople of the city of Centurion, and to inform them about the purposes and goals of the Centurion Chamber of Commerce. It will be there to help promote and protect the interests of it's members, as well as the interests of the city and and the community as a whole. We want to protect and encourage business and commerce in the city of Centurion
